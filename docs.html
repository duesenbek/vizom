Getting started with Vizom.

Our full documentation is still rolling out. Meanwhile, here is a high-level overview of how to prompt, collaborate, and ship AI-generated visuals safely inside Vizom.

1. Prompt the generator

Describe the data source, desired chart, and audience. Vizom infers fields, metrics, and annotations automatically.

2. Calibrate the layout

Use smart presets for reporting, investor decks, or executive dashboards. All exports are brand-ready.

3. Share instantly

Publish secure links or export to PNG/PDF. Role-based permissions keep stakeholders aligned.
